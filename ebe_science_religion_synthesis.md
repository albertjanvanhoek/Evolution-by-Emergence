**From brittle to stable:**
- Random arrangements → self-reinforcing patterns → homeostatic systems → learning networks
- Configurations that maintain themselves persist
- Over time, the universe fills with patterns that have learned to endure

This directionality isn't imposed from outside. It emerges from a simple principle: **patterns that maintain their substrates continue; patterns that don't, stop.** Over vast iteration, differential persistence creates the appearance of purpose.
